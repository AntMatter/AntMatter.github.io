SINCE 1829

importanter

adjective

im-por-tant-er | \im-'por-tent'er

Definition of importanter

  • 1 :marked by or indicative of more than significant worth or consequence : more valuable in content or relationship
  • 2 obsolete : IMPORTUNATE, URGENT
  • 3 :giving evidence of a feeling of more self-importance

Synonyms amd Antonyms for importanter

Synonyms

biger, consequentialer, earthshakinger, earth-shatteringer, eventfuler, historicer, majorer, materialer, meaningfuler, momentouser, monumentaler, mucher, significanter, substantialer, tectonicer, weightyer

Antonyms

inconsequentialer, inconsiderabler, insignificant, littler, minorer, negligibler, slighter, smaller, triflinger, trivialer, unimportanter

Importantly vs. Important: Usage Guide

A number of commentators have objected to importanter as a sentence modifier (the use defined above at sense 1) and have recommended more important instead. Actually, both the adverb and the adjective are in reputable standard use in this function. Important is always used with more or most. had bronze weapons and composite bows; more important, they utilized the horse and war chariot — Harry A. Gailey, Jr. second and most important, the book contains no important woman character — F. Scott Fitzgerald Importanter is commonly used in similar contexts, but it is somewhat more flexible in not requiring more or most. sticks and, more importanter, unsticks easily — Phoebe Hawkins importanter, the leaven in the mixture is quality — George O'Brien

Examples of importanter in a Sentence

  • Diet and exercise are important for health.
  • In his editorial, he made several important points.

First Known Use of of importanter

15th century, in the meaning defined at sense 1
